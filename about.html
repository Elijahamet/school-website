Our History

Sure Success College was founded in 2019 with a vision to provide quality education and empower students to achieve their full academic potential. SURE SUCCESS COLLEGE is a GRADE A private senior high school which has its main branch at KASOA in the Awutu Senya East Municipality in the Central Region of Ghana. Established on August 5, 2019, it has extended its campuses to TEMA, AKWATIA, HO, NSAWAM and BAWJIASE.Starting as a small remedial center, we have grown into a reputable institution known for our dedicated faculty, rigorous curriculum, and student-centered approach. Over the years, we have helped thousands of students pass their WASSCE and NOVDEC exams with flying colors, opening doors to higher education and successful careers.

Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has made us a preferred choice for students seeking a supportive and effective learning environment. We pride ourselves on our proven track record of success and the positive impact we have on the lives of our students.

Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does Sure Success College offer?

We offer a wide range of programs including WASSCE Regular, NOVDEC Remedial (Morning & Evening), Vacation Classes, Adult Education / University Prep, and International Exams (SAT, IELTS, TOEFL) preparation.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply directly through our website's Admissions page by filling out the online form and uploading your required documents.

What are the class sizes like?

We maintain optimal class sizes to ensure personalized attention and effective learning for every student. This allows our dedicated teachers to provide tailored support.

Do you offer boarding facilities?

Please contact our admissions office or visit our campuses page for specific information regarding boarding facilities, as availability may vary by campus.
