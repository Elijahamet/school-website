Registration Guide

Follow these simple steps to complete your registration process:

  1. Fill the Online Form: Complete the application form on this page with accurate details.
  2. Submit via WhatsApp: Click the "Submit Application via WhatsApp" button. This will open WhatsApp with your pre-filled application details.
  3. Send Message: Confirm and send the message on WhatsApp.
  4. Review & Follow-up: Our admissions team will review your application and contact you soon.
  5. Interview/Assessment (if applicable): You may be contacted for a brief interview or assessment.
  6. Admission Offer: Successful applicants will receive an admission offer via email or phone.
  7. Payment & Enrollment: Complete the payment of fees to secure your enrollment.
  8. Orientation: Attend our orientation program to get familiar with the college environment.

For any assistance during the application process, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form (online)
  • Copy of Birth Certificate or Ghana Card
  • Recent Passport-sized Photographs (2)
  • Previous Academic Transcripts/Result Slips (e.g., BECE, WASSCE)
  • Recommendation Letter (optional, for certain programs)

*Specific requirements may vary based on the program.
